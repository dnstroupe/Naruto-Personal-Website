About Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is a fictional character in the anime and manga franchise Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. Serving as the eponymous protagonist of the series, he is a young ninja from the fictional village of Konoha. The hardships of his early life caused Naruto to dream of becoming the village's leader, the Hokage, in order to gain recognition from his peers.

Throughout the series, Naruto is depicted as a cheerful, hyperactive, and optimistic individual, but also shows a lack of knowledge and understanding for certain situations. He harbors an enormous, optimistic drive, and his desire to become Hokage is a significant part of his character throughout the series.
